At TechDev, we stand behind the quality of our products and services. This Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returns, refunds, and cancellations.

Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most of our services. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your purchase.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Subscription Services

You may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial subscription purchase if:

  • This is your first time subscribing to the service
  • You have not extensively used the service features
  • You contact us within 30 days of the purchase date
  • Your account is in good standing

One-Time Purchases

One-time software licenses and products are eligible for return within 30 days if:

  • The product has not been downloaded or activated
  • You can demonstrate a defect or incompatibility issue
  • The product does not match the description provided

Custom Development Services

Custom development projects may be eligible for partial refunds based on:

  • Stage of completion
  • Milestones delivered
  • Terms outlined in your service agreement

2. Non-Refundable Items and Services

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription renewals (automatic billing cycles after the first 30 days)
  • Usage-based charges for consumed resources (compute hours, storage, API calls)
  • Professional services already rendered
  • Training sessions that have been delivered
  • Domain registrations and third-party services
  • Products purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Services where you have violated our Terms of Service

To initiate a return or refund request, follow these steps:

  2. Provide Information: Include your account email, order number, and reason for return

Once your return is approved:

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. We cannot issue refunds to different payment methods or accounts.

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds based on:
